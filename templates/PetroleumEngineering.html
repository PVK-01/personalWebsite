Logging, directional drilling fascinates me. So, I was ble to score a perfect 10 Semester GPA during my 6th semester where they both were the main subjects. I have done the below projects related to oil and gas during my under-graduation:



Projects:
Analytical and computer-based Simulation of Cyclic Steam Stimulation
  • Fabricated a computer-based (CMG Stars) simulation model of a reservoir and found the resultant heat injected and the production at varying injection criterion (steam injection rate, injection volume, injection interval, steam Quality).
  • Analyzed different analytical models predicting the recovery, heat distribution, number of cycles, etc., of the cyclic steam stimulated wells.


Drilling of Shale Gas Wells
  • Studied different drilling Criterion like drill string design, drilling mud design, drilling trajectory design for drilling shale gas wells.
  • The main issue was to find the original regressors that really affected the dependent variable, which was price.
  • Using a model made up of a stack of 2 different machine learning techniques an accuracy of 96% was achieved on the test set. The main goal of the project, to maintain less overfitting, was achieved.
